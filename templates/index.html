About This Event

The inaugural Bitcoin Airdrop event was launched by the MIT Bitcoin Club in 2014 after the club raised 100k in donations to give out to incoming students. The McGill Students Cryptocurrency Club continued the tradition the next year during their frosh week after receiving generous donations from the blockchain community. Since then, the Bitcoin Airdrop has become a yearly tradition of the Blockchain Education Network and is held in cities across the world to help region leaders launch their community at the beginning of each school year.

Join the Blockchain Education Network in welcoming new members to our community and teaching them how to use digital currency.

So far, we have introduced blockchain and digital currency to thousands of people in countries across the world. We believe that digital currency and blockchains represent a paradigm shift in society and we want to support our region leaders to share this exciting technology with their community. About bitJob

Reshaping Students' Freedom to Earn a Living

bitJob is an ambitious and social project, based on Blockchain technology.
Aimed at revolutionizing the way students make a living and gain occupational experience while still studying. bitJob will give students the opportunity to receive immediate payment for their abilities from professional employers and to sharpen their skills while enriching their dynamic résumé, anytime & anywhere!

FAQ

Here is a list of answers to frequently asked questions about the event.

  • 01. General FAQ
  • 02. Host FAQ
Why you give out bitcoin every year?

From our experience, the best way to understand how digital currency and blockchain works is to try it.

How much bitcoin is each person going to receive?

Each person is going to receive ~$5 worth of bitcoin. There will be additional ways to win more bitcoin.

What if I want to get involved or sponsor this years Airdrop?

The best way to get involved in BEN events is to join our Slack channel. If you want to sponsor this, please email us contact@blockchainedu.org.

What can the people who receive bitcoin do with it?

We will provide a selection of items available for bitcoin. People can also purchase anything on Amazon for a discount using bitcoin with services such as Purse.io.

How do you have the bitcoin to give out?

We receive generous donations from the blockchain community and have amazing title sponsor bitJob, supporting sponsors and media partners who make the event possible.

What if there is no event at my campus or in my region?

Then we encourage you to host a drop! Airdrops are led by changemakers just like you who want to make an impact in their local community.

Can you give out bitcoin online?

Yes, we will have opportunities for you to give out bitcoin online too.

If you still have unanswered questions, please contact us.
