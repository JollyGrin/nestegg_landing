What's NestEgg?

An altnerative approach to pensions by reducing your future cost of living.
Invest directly in infrastructure that provides you monthly dividends.

We might not know how much money we’ll need in the future, but we do know we'll need energy, housing, care, transportation and so on.

NestEgg enables individuals to directly own pieces of the sustainable infrastructure that powers their future. This would consist of ownership in green energy assets, houses and so on. Each individual would receive the value created from one’s investment, traceable to the percentage of ownership in the asset.
